Let's be honest, working in the lab requires wasting lot of time between data-treating, and filling your own lab notebook. The Electronic notebook softwares around are overcomplicated slow 90's pieces of crap. SLAB simiplifies all that. Oh, and make it blizzardly fast.

With Slab, you take pictures, mail data, and write equations fast and easily using a markdown editor and latex. Or if you can't bother learning the synthax, you'll be doing great with our visual editor.

And why not be able to freehand draw to jot that damn flux capacitor sketch you need?

And finally, let's not forget that a lab notebook is a legal document. All the copies of your files are safely stored and automatically synced among all your devices, and subject to version control. Yes, we are sorry, but you cannot go and edit your old documents and pretend you deserve the nobel prize.

FAQ

Why do I need an electronic laboratory notebook?
Right now, you don't need it. With what there's around is just a waste of time. But Slab gives you all the candy - you can write and sketch and shoot and mail data during your experiments, and keep track of your stuff, on laptops and smartphones and mainframes. And eventually you can share it with your boss. Eventually.
